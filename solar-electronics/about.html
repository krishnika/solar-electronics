Best Home Appliances About Us

We started in 1989 as the manufacturers of black and white TVs. Along with manufacturing, we were an active supplier of TV parts to national and international brands. Throughout these years we have consistently and quickly adapted to the changes in the market and technology to increase the profits of both, our distributors and us. We cater to the demands of 30-40% of the Madhya Pradesh market and have recently expanded to Gujrat as well.

After working with the leaders in the space for 30 long years, we have launched our new brand, Bluechip, targetted especially to fulfill the demands of the Indian customers. Our brand is associated with 3 core principles: High-Quality products, Nominal pricing, and high customer satisfaction. Our products range from TV/LEDs to Coolers, Set-top boxes, and Portable Atta Chakki. We plan to expand to other products in near future.
